Hello!

I am a designer from Vancouver, BC. Focused on user experience design, I am passionate about exploring human behaviour and relationships in the context of art and design. Nurtured through professional experience and academic learning, I can identify and frame problems and solve them through critical thinking, experimentation, and evaluation. I enjoy learning and mastering new design skills and tools, and bringing my expertise to interdisciplinary collaborations.

As a child, my Sunday mornings consisted of watching Art Attack and Bob Ross. Enamored by the sound of scissors cutting cardboard and pallet knives scraping canvas, I could not resist but do the same. In my Grade 11 Desktop Publishing class, I was introduced to Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign. Suddenly, the evidence for my compulsion to create appeared in both physical and digital form and it has not stopped since. Have a look at my artwork on Tumblr.
