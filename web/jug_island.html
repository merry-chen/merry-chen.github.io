Belcarra

Jug Island Beach Trail

Situated at the entrance to Indian Arm, a glacially deepened fjord, this park features forests of cedar, hemlock and fir. Steep slopes and a heavily-indented shoreline make for spectacular scenery. Swimming is popular in Sasamat Lake.

Trail Information

Time 2.5 hours return

Distance 5.5 km

Elevation Gain 100 m

Difficulty Medium

Season Year-Round

Dogs Permitted Yes, On-Leash
