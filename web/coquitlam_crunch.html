Coquitlam

Coquitlam Crunch

A popular fitness challenge for many local residents. Hikers and fitness walkers enjoy the natural setting and spectacular views of the city on this uphill climb. The trail consists of more than 400 timber stairs that extends to the top of Westwood Plateau.

Trail Information

Time 30 min

Distance 2.2 km

Elevation Gain 242 m

Difficulty Hard

Season Year-Round

Dogs Permitted Yes, On-Leash
