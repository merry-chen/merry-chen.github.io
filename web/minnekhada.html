Coquitlam

Minnekhada Regional Park

Minnekhada Regional Park is over 200 hectares of forest, marsh and rocky outcrops. Its network of quiet trails are rich with places to hike and to watch for wildlife. It is also the site of Minnekhada Lodge, a picturesque example of the country estate lifestyle of the 1930s, with spectacular views of the Pitt River.

Trail Information

Time 2.5 hours return

Distance 10 km

Elevation Gain 180 m

Difficulty Easy

Season Year-Round

Dogs Permitted Yes, On-Leash
