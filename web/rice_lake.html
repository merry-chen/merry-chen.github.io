North Vancouver

Rice Lake Loop

A trail circles around the lake and has several different viewpoints of the lake. You will often find that fishing is popular here as the lake is stocked with trout each year. Unfortunately swimming is no longer permitted in Rice Lake.

Trail Information

Time 30 min

Distance 2.5 km

Elevation Flat

Difficulty Easy

Season Year-Round

Dogs Permitted No
