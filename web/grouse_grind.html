North Vancouver

Grouse Grind

The Grouse Grind is a 2.9 km trail up the face of Grouse Mountain. This trail is very challenging, consisting of 2,830 steps. Keep in mind that there is a wide range of mountaintop trails that might better suit the average hiker.

Trail Information

Time 1.5 to 2 hours

Distance 2.9 km

Elevation 900 ft

Difficulty Hard

Season Spring, Summer, Fall

Dogs Permitted No
