Before product redesign

Customer Journey Map Workshop

To better understand the user's experience and identify the shortcomings of the current system, I decided to run a customer journey mapping workshop. I gathered the CEO, CTO, Director of Sales, Director of Software Engineering, PM, BA, and the Customer Success Team in one room to tell me the story of the customer’s experience: from a first-time user to an expert return user. The X axis lists the various steps a new and expert scheduler typically takes to publish a schedule. The Y axis addresses the “feeling”, “thinking”, and “doing” of a scheduler at each touchpoint.
